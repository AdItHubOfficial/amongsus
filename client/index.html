    How to Play

    Crew: complete every task on the ISV Meridian, or find the impostors and vote them out at meetings.

    Impostors: eliminate the crew without getting caught. Fake tasks, slip through vents, sabotage systems and lock doors.

    Meetings: report a body (R) or hit the emergency console in the Atrium. Discuss, then vote — the majority is ejected from the airlock.

    Sabotage: reactor and oxygen failures must be fixed before the timer runs out or the impostors win. Lights shrink crew vision; comms hide tasks.

    Ghosts: eliminated crew keep helping — finish your tasks from beyond, and chat with other ghosts.

    Controls: WASD/arrows move · E use · R report · Q kill/sabotage · V vent · F ability · M map · T emotes · Esc menu.
